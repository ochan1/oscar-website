About Me

Greetings! My name is Oscar Chan.
I am a 5th Year Masters student in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at the University of California, Berkeley. I was formerly a Pasadena City College student, transferred to EECS at the University of California, Berkeley. My research involves Human-Computer Interaction and Applied Compilers for the Snap! programming language, used for Computer Science education
I am a hard-working individual who always uses trial and error and constant self-motivation in order to complete the task on hand. This is evidenced by my several recognitions from classes and fellow peers!
I am a fast learner, willing and excited to learn!
